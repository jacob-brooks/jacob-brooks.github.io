Project 3: Analyzing the Impact of Reviews on Airbnb Booking Rates

Authors: Edith Tache, Cali Hugelen, Jacob Brooks, Remington Webb
Season: Summer, 2024

Introduction

Objective: Examine how reviews impact Airbnb booking rates in California.

Approach: Utilize Kaggle dataset to analyze the relationship between review attributes and booking rates through data cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA), and predictive modeling.

Dataset: "AirBnB Listings in California" from Kaggle includes attributes such as price, review scores, and host details.

Business Problem/Hypothesis

Research Questions:

  • Impact of the number of reviews on booking rates.
  • Impact of review quality on booking rates.
  • Key factors in reviews contributing to higher booking rates.

Hypotheses:

  • Number of reviews significantly affects booking rates.
  • Higher review scores correlate with higher booking rates.

Methods/Analysis

Data Cleaning and Preparation

Dropped unnecessary columns, handled missing values, and created features like booking rate.

Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

Analyzed outliers, review count distribution, and review scores' impact using visualizations like box plots and scatter plots.

Feature Engineering

Encoded categorical variables and normalized features.

Model Training and Evaluation

Models: Random Forest Regressor and XGBoost Regressor.

Training: Both models were trained and evaluated for predictive performance. Hyperparameter tuning was conducted using RandomizedSearchCV and GridSearchCV.

Metrics: Mean Squared Error (MSE) and R² Score were used to evaluate models.

Results

Model Performance:

  • Random Forest Regressor:
    • Pre-Tuning: MSE = 0.0778, R² = 0.4356.
    • Post-Tuning: MSE = 0.0794, R² = 0.4238.
  • XGBoost Regressor:
    • Pre-Tuning: MSE = 0.1115, R² = 0.1913.
    • Post-Tuning: MSE = 0.1050, R² = 0.2380.

Key Findings: Random Forest performed better than XGBoost, indicating robustness but with room for improvement. Review quantity did not directly impact booking rates, whereas review quality had a weak positive correlation.

Recommendations/Ethical Considerations

Strategies for Hosts:

  • Optimize pricing strategies.
  • Improve host acceptance rates and responsiveness.
  • Enhance property features.
  • Leverage reviews and guest feedback.

Ethical Considerations: Ensure transparency, accuracy in listings, and protection of guest data.

Limitations and Future Work

Limitations:

  • Data quality and completeness issues.
  • Regional specificity (California).
  • Snapshot nature of data.

Future Work:

  • Expand dataset to multiple regions.
  • Conduct dynamic and time-series analysis.
  • Explore advanced predictive models.

Conclusion

Summary: Review quality slightly correlates with booking rates, but price is the most significant factor. Effective pricing strategies, high property standards, and prompt host responses are crucial for optimizing booking rates.
